Release Notes 2015

The 2015 release of LS One includes LS One Express, the cloud-based version of LS One.

The biggest change in LS One 2015 is the streamlining of the setup and configuration of LS One to make it more cloud-friendly and easier to use. The aim of this change is to provide our partners with great tools and opportunities to expand their offerings for various verticals in different countries.
