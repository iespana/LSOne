Forms

Operation group, Forms, has three main tasks:

  • To create and maintain the layout of the different forms that are printed by the LS One POS.
  • To import and export form layouts.
  • To view and create form types and maintain user defined types.

These tasks are processed in Forms and labels.

To open Form Layouts select Store setup > Forms and labels > Form layouts
