Customer Operations

Customer Search

It is possible to add the customer into the sale any time. To do this, use the operation Customer Search.

The customer search dialog is shown where you can see the customer's account, alias, name and address. The invoice customer will also be shown if any customer has an invoice customer set.

The user can scroll down or up to find the customer and also search by inserting/typing on the text field. Searching will try to match customer's information but also the invoice customer's informations.

If the new customer was only saved locally, when the site service connection is established, the button will become enabled and the user has the option to transfer him to the head office.

If the customer is not on the list, the user can add a new customer by pressing the "+" button.

 

By adding the customer into the sale, the sale receives their prices and discounts even though the sale is not be charged into their account.

In HYPER AURORA, Customer Search can be accessed through the miscellaneous button.

In AURORA FASHION the customer search can be used directly in the customer panel.

Customer Transactions

Use this operation to look for customer transactions. Prior to using this operation, the customer has to be added into the sale. If no customer has been added into the sale and this operation is used, a warning message is displayed.

Note: This operation works only for terminals that are defined as independent terminals.

Customer Account Deposit

Use this operation for the POS to accept store payments from customers. A Customer Account Deposit window opens. Select a customer, enter the deposit amount and select enter/OK.
