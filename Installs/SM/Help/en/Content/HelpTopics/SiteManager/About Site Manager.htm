About LS One Site Manager

The LS One Site Manager not only gives you complete control over your business but it allows you to give your customers options by reducing the time it takes to handle all the information that you need to reduce waste and increase success. The LS One Site Manager is a light-weight back-office system based on .NET for single and multiple site locations. Edit and configure items, customers, store settings and options as well as your POS layouts to meet your business needs all in one place.

Logs and Reports

Audit logs and reports give you a clear overview of sales. Customer functions such as the ledger give you an up-to-date view of transactions and payments.

Inventory Overview

The inventory options give you a clear overview of stock history, stock levels, and purchase orders. You can even make transfer orders to different sites to make sure you never run out.

Set Reminders

Infocodes allow you to set item modifiers on the POS, cross-sell or set reminders to the POS user at a particular point in a transaction.

Product Variations

Variations allow you to have one product with many variations, such as style, size and color, the possibilities are endless.

Organize Items

Organize items within departments and groups, allowing you to access them with one click to create discounts, set infocodes and tax groups.

Navigate Quickly

Never spend time looking frantically for what you want in the Site Manager, with a search tool, smart links and quick navigation buttons you will find what you need in an instant.
