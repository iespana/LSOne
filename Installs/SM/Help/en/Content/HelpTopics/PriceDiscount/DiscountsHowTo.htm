How to setup discounts

LS One offers the possibility of setting up a wide variety of discount offers and promotions. Below are some examples of discount offers and deals. Use them to help you understand how the discount functionality in LS One works and how the discounts are created.

3 for 2 (all same item)

A discount where the customer buys three pieces of the same item but only pays for two.

  1. Go to Retail > Price/discount
  2. Select Mix and match
  3. Press to create a new mix and match offer.
  4. Choose a name for the offer in the Description field. For example ‘3 for 2‘.
  5. Under Price/discount choose Least expensive
  6. Set ‘Number of least expensive lines‘ to 1, because the cheapest product should be for free.
  7. Press OK
  8. Press in the lower list Mix and match lines
  9. Choose item in the item field.
  10. Press to the right of the Line group field.
  11. In the Mix and Match line groups dialog press
  12. Key in description. It can be anything.
  13. Enter 3 in Number of items needed, because three items need to be sold at the POS to trigger this offer
  14. Choose color group.
  15. Press OK to close the Mix and Match line group dialog.
  16. Press Close to close the Mix and match line groups dialog.
  17. Select the line group you created in the Line group drop down
  18. Press OK to close the Mix and Match line dialog and add the item to the discount.
  19. With the new offer selected press the Enable button

Now your new offer should be available and active on the POS

    6. Key in description for an example B.

20% discount of all dairy (Discount offer)

An discount percentage given to all items in a retail group.

  1. Go to Retail > Price/discount
  2. Select Discount offers
  3. Press to create a new Discount offer.
  4. Choose a name for the offer in the Description field. For example ‘Dairy 20%‘.
  5. Set Discount to 20.

  6. Press OK to create the discount offer.

  7. Add the retail group to the offer:
    1. Press under Discount offer lines
    2. Set Type to Retail group.
    3. Choose the retail group (Dairy for this example).
    4. Press OK to close the Discount offer line dialog and add the retail group to the discount
  8. With the new offer selected press the Enable button

 

To trigger the offer:

Open the POS and sell an item from this retail group and verify the discount is 20%

Sell for over $50 and get a $2 total discount

 

  1. Go to Retail > Price/discount
  2. Select Customer discount groups
  3. Set Show to ‘Total discount group‘
  4. Press under list of groups
  5. Enter a Description for the total discount group.
  6. Press OK to create the new total discount group.
  7. Press under Customers tab (the lower list) and select customers for the group. (Repeat as necessary)
  8. Select Discounts tab (lower list)
  9. Press under in the Discounts tab to create a discount
  10. In the Total discount dialog:

    1. Set Amount to 50

    2. Set Discount amount to 2

    3. Press OK

To trigger the offer:

Open the POS and select a customer from the total discount group and sell items for $50 or more. Verify the discount is $2.

 

Note that the total discounts need to be enabled in the price discount settings in administrative options to allow these discounts to be calculated at the POS.

Go to Tools > Administrative options > Active price/Discount and check in total discount for both customers and customer groups.
