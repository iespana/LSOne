Working with Offers and Promotions

The method of working with offers follows Site Manager’s general rules. Each window has its edit, add and delete buttons. When a new entry is added or edited, the card for the entry opens in a pop-up window.

The Promotion/offer view:

An Offer’s Creation Process:

  1. Press the Add button in the upper part of the screen.
  2. Enter the description and configure the offer in the pop-up card. Close the window.
  3. The offer has the status Disabled.
  4. Select the offer.
  5. Select Add or Edit buttons in the lower part of the screen to add the offer lines that trigger the offer.
  6. Select the offer in the upper part of the screen and click on button Activate to activate the offer. Its status is now enabled.
    Note:     An offer cannot be edited when in status Enabled. You have to click on the Deactivate button to edit it.
