Priority for Offers

The offers, Discount offer, Multibuy and Mix and match, all have a Priority field.

The priority of the offer controls which offer is triggered if the item exists in more than one offer of the same category.

A lower priority number gives the offer a higher priority.

Priorities of all discounts

You can see all periodic discounts ordered by priority in the periodic discounts view.

In the ribbon go to Item > Priority

In this view you can reorder the priority of discounts with the and buttons.

Example of how to use Discount Offer:

  1. Create a new Floral Blouse offer.
  2. Set the offer’s discount to 10%.
  3. Enter the priority at 60.
  4. In the offer line, enter item Floral Blouse Tim –n Tina Wear, ID 40120.
  5. Make the Tim-n Wear offer 25% off and set the Floral Blouse offer to Enabled.
  6. Go into the LS POS.
  7. Sell item Floral Blouse Tim –n Tina Wear or ID 40120.
  8. The Floral Blouse offer is triggered as it has a lower priority.
  9. When the priority of the Floral Blouse offer is changed to 600 and the item is sold again the other offer will be triggered instead.
  10. Remember that to change an offer you have to select it in the Site Manager and deactivate it. After correcting it, you have to activate it again to make it available on the LS POS.
