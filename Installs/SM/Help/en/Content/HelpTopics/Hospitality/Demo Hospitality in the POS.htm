1.6 Demo Hospitality in the POS

This chapter shows the demo data Hospitality views in the POS. Bear in mind that permissions can be set so that operations are not accessible to all POS users. Equally, the buttons, button grid layouts, items and many more configurations are fully customizable to meet your business needs. Please see chapter 7 LS One POS Hospitality Functionality 7 LS One POS Hospitality Functionality for a more detailed description of POS setup, operations and functionalities.


The demo data is really useful to help you explore the hospitality capabilities. It can also be used and customized to your needs, saving you a lot of work in setup.


To control general settings, table button layout, split bill and transfer lines go to Hospitality type in the Site Manager.
