Serial Number in Variation Groups

The serial number is additional information which can be required to register with the item number at the time the item is sold.

In the Site Manager it is possible to use a feature in the variation group to enable or disable the serial number.

Items that have a serial number should be registered and configured with a variation group that has the serial number enabled.

Variation groups can be configured to have:

  • One, two or all three of the variation types checked;
  • Only the serial number;
  • A mixture of the variations types and serial number.

In the above figure one variation group; Serial, has been added into the list. Variation group Serial is enabled for the serial number

Variation group card:

When items in LS POS that have the variation group enabled are sold, a pop-up window appears asking you to enter the serial number. Serial numbers can be a combination of numbers and letters.

The serial number comes into a separated line below the item and is registered into the database.

 

This topic applies to version 2015.1 and older versions of LS One.
