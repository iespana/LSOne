Variation Group for an Item

When a new item is created, one of the mandatory fields is Variation group. The possibility No‑Dimension is used for all items that do not have any variation. For variation items one of the variation groups has to be selected.

Note:

  • The variation types available are decided by the variation group that you select. Each variation group can have zero to three variation types available.

The variation group field is on the General tab on the Retail item card:
