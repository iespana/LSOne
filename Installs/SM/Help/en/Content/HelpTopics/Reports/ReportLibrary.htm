Report library

Here you can select reports to import to your LS One Site Manager. All reports are divided into the following categories:

Category: Description:
Comparison Reports that compare selected periods between years. For an example compare total sales for January this year and last year.
Item Item sales reports for all items in different formats.
Sales Sales reports constrained to different variables, such as employee, terminal, hour, vendor and top/bottom sales report
Financial All financial reports
Customer Customer ledger reports
Inventory Reports to help with stock take and reordering
Other transaction Reports of all issued gift cards, credit memos and returns.

 

Below you can download reports.

You can test importing any report into your system to see if it works for you, nothing will be destroyed. And if it does not work you can just remove the report again.

See How to add report for further information on how to import the report to your system.

Item reports
Report: Description: Created for version: Preview: Download:
Item sale by item This reports lists all items sold over a period of time for summed up for one or more locations. It lists the retail group the item belongs to, the qty sold, total of discounts given and the net amount for each item.  
Item sale by item/price

Same as Item sale by item but the it shows one line per price the item has been sold for. So if the price has changed during the period or if the price has been overridden at the POS the report has multiple lines for that item.

 
Item sale by item/price without variants

Same as the one above but it does not include columns for the Size, Color or Style and will sum up numbers for the items including all variants.

  
Item sales by hour This report shows the sales of a selected item by hour over a selected period.  
Item sales and cost per item by retail group This reports shows a list of all sold items over a selected period and displays the price, cost, discounts and net amount.  
Item sales by vendor This report shows total sales for selected vendor over a selected period  
Item sales most/least sold This report shows the best/least sold items by quantity or amount
