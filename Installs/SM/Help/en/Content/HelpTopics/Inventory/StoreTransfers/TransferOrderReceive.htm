How to receive inventory transfer orders

  1. Select General setup > Inventory > Transfer orders
  2. A transfer order list view is displayed
  3. Open the To receive tab in the upper list view
  4. Select the transfer order from the list of transfer orders that is displayed
  5. Click the Receive button
