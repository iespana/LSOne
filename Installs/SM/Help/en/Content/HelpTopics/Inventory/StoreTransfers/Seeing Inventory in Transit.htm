Seeing Inventory in Transit

If the Site Manager is set to head office you have the option of seeing the inventory that is in transit. Go to Site Manager > General setup > Inventory > Inventory in transit

Using the sample stores from the previous chapter we will create a transfer of 100 kg of bananas and 50 kg of apples from the High inventory store to the Low inventory store.

Image: Inventory in Transit View

 

After the inventory is sent from the High inventory store but before it is received at the Low inventory store it is in transit and we can keep track of inventory that is in transit when we are at head office.

Open the Transfer orders view by highlighting the line and selecting the View inventory transfer button.

Image: Transfer orders view

Here we see a single line for the inventory transfer order that is in transit. We see when it was sent, to and from locations and the number of units that are in transit.
