Create an Inventory Transfer Order Without a Request

The woman responsible for stock status at the High inventory store notices that they have more bananas and apples than they can possibly sell in the next couple of days. She goes to the store manager and informs him.

He calls his colleague at the Low inventory store to ask him if he could use either of these excess items. It turns out that the Low inventory store is running low on stock and could use some bananas and apples.

The High inventory store manager goes into the Site Manager and creates an inventory transfer order with the receiving store being the Low inventory store. With the help of the stock master they decide that their surplus is about 100 kg of bananas and 50 kg of apples. He adds these items to the transfer and sends it.

The apples and bananas are sent and delivered to the to the Low inventory store.

The manager of the Low inventory store clicks on the Receive button and moves the inventory transfer order from the To receive tab, checking that it moves to the Finished tab.
