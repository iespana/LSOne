Goods Receiving Menus

Goods receiving can be done using a mobile device like an mobile phone running iOS or Android. This enables the user to do the receiving standing next to the delivery in the warehouse for an example.

Preparation:

To be able to do goods receiving in the LS Omni Mobile Inventory app first the purchase order needs to exist.

Read here on how to create purchase orders.

How to do goods receiving in the app:

In the layout provided in the Omni data pack you find the Goods receiving menu on the startup menu. It includes the operations; Goods receiving and Post goods receiving.
If you have created your own button setup you need to add a button with the Stock counting operation assigned to it.

 

  1. In the goods receiving you will find a list of all open purchase orders for your site.
  2. Select the purchase order to receive by pressing the line
  3. The purchase order header details are displayed on the screen.
  4. press open
  5. The goods receiving document is displayed with all the purchase order lines and their ordered quantity.
  6. If any item on the list has not been fully received or if you need over-receive and item you can edit the quantity or clear any edits of each line by pressing the button on the right side of the line.
  7. Lines can also be added to the goods receiving document by searching for it by description, ID or by scanning a barcode.
  8. At any time during your receiving you can press the save button to Save your edits and continue later.
  9. When done with editing the list according to your received quantities press the Send button.
  10. When the send button has been pressed the goods receiving for this purchase order has been updated and can be viewed in the Site Manager.
  11. The goods receiving document has not been posted at this point and can still be edited. To post you can either post it from the Site Manager or go to the Post goods receiving in the LS Omni Mobile Inventory app.

 

How to post goods receiving in the app:

In the layout provided in the Omni data pack you find the Goods receiving menu on the startup menu. It includes the operations; Goods receiving and Post goods receiving.
If you have created your own button setup you need to add a button with the Stock counting operation assigned to it.

 

When a Goods receiving document has been edited and sent in the LS Omni Inventory app it moves to the list of goods receiving documents to be posted.

  1. Open the Post goods receiving to find a list of all goods receiving documents that have been edited and sent in the app.
  2. The goods receiving lines can still be edited and lines added in the same way as in the goods receiving.
  3. When all lines are up to date and the goods receiving document can be posted to update the inventory then press the Send button.
