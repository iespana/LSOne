Central Inventory Lookup

Central inventory lookup lets you look up the inventory status for items from a central location.

The Site Service has a functionality which enables the operations Suspend and Recall transaction to work centrally.

To use the Central inventory lookup in the system:

  • The LS Site Service needs to be installed and configured;
  • The Store needs to have the functionality Use central suspensions active in its Site Service profile;
  • The LS POS needs to have a button configured with the operation Inventory lookup.

In the LS POS Demo Data, the Inventory Lookup button is under Miscellaneous.
