Inventory Journals

Inventory journals group together:

  • Inventory adjustments
  • Stock reservation
  • Parked inventory

 

Definitions

  • Inventory adjustments - increases or decreases made to inventory to match an item's actual on-hand quantity
  • Stock reservations - used to ensure the stock availability when delivering a Sales Order
  • Parked inventory - contains items that are damaged, returned, in a windows display, sent to a photographer for a commercial shoot or any other case

Permissions

Access to each type of inventory journal is provided by two permissions:

  • for current store
  • for all stores
